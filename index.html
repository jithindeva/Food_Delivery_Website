About Food Buds

Food Buds is a next-generation food delivery, instant grocery, and gourmet dine-out booking platform built to satisfy your every craving instantly.

Founded in 2026, our mission is to connect food lovers with premium culinary experiences in their local neighborhoods and on the move. Whether you are seeking a quick 10-minute Bolt snack delivery, a budget-friendly 99-store meal, fresh groceries delivered directly to your doorstep via Instamart, or custom-cooked dishes delivered directly to your train coach seat, Food Buds has you covered.
